2. Free Shipping

We offer FREE Standard Shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States!

  • Automatically applied at checkout when your subtotal exceeds $50
  • Applies to standard shipping only
  • Valid for shipments within the continental U.S., Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories
  • Cannot be combined with other shipping promotions

3. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days:

  • Orders placed Monday-Friday before 2PM EST: Processed same business day
  • Orders placed after 2PM EST: Processed next business day
  • Weekend orders: Processed on the following Monday
  • Holiday orders: May experience slight delays during peak seasons

You'll receive an email confirmation when your order is placed and another when it ships with tracking information.

4. Shipping Locations

Domestic Shipping (United States)

We currently ship to:

  • All 50 states
  • Washington D.C.
  • Puerto Rico
  • U.S. Virgin Islands
  • Guam
  • American Samoa
  • Northern Mariana Islands

International Shipping

We do not currently offer international shipping outside of U.S. territories. We're working to expand our shipping options in the future!

PO Boxes and Military Addresses

  • PO Boxes: We ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS
  • APO/FPO/DPO: We proudly ship to military addresses at standard domestic rates

5. Order Tracking

Stay informed about your order every step of the way:

  • Tracking number provided via email once your order ships
  • Track your order on our Track Order page
  • Real-time updates from the carrier
  • Delivery notifications when your package arrives

If you don't receive tracking information within 3 business days, please contact us at help@lunarisshop.online

6. Shipping Carriers

We partner with trusted carriers to ensure safe delivery:

  • USPS (United States Postal Service)
  • UPS (United Parcel Service)
  • FedEx

The carrier is selected based on your location and shipping method chosen. We use the most efficient option to get your order to you quickly.

7. Delivery Issues

Delayed Packages

If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date:

  • Check the tracking information for updates
  • Allow 1-2 additional business days (carriers sometimes experience delays)
  • Contact us if it's been more than 2 business days past the estimated delivery

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you didn't receive it:

  1. Check with household members and neighbors
  2. Look around your property (back door, garage, mailbox)
  3. Contact the carrier to open an investigation
  4. Wait 24-48 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
  5. Contact us at help@lunarisshop.online - we'll help resolve the issue

We'll work with you to file a claim and send a replacement or issue a refund.

Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the packaging and damaged items
  • Contact us within 48 hours at help@lunarisshop.online
  • Include your order number and photos
  • We'll send a replacement immediately or issue a full refund

8. Address Changes

Need to change your shipping address?

  • Before shipment: Contact us immediately at help@lunarisshop.online and we'll update it
  • After shipment: Contact the carrier directly with your tracking number to request a delivery change (fees may apply)

Important: We cannot guarantee address changes once an order has shipped. Please ensure your address is correct at checkout.

9. Refused or Returned Packages

If a package is refused or returned to us:

  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • We'll refund the product cost minus return shipping fees
  • If the return was due to an incorrect address provided, a $10 processing fee applies

10. Peak Season Shipping

During peak shopping seasons (holidays, Black Friday, etc.):

  • Processing times may be extended by 1-2 business days
  • Carrier delays are more common due to high volume
  • We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery
  • Consider Express or Overnight shipping for guaranteed delivery dates

We'll send email updates if we anticipate any delays.

11. Weather and Natural Disasters

In cases of severe weather or natural disasters:

  • Carrier delays are beyond our control
  • Deliveries may be suspended in affected areas for safety
  • We'll keep you updated via email
  • No additional charges for delayed deliveries due to weather

12. Multiple Item Orders

If you order multiple items:

  • We try to ship all items together when possible
  • Some items may ship separately from different warehouses
  • You'll receive separate tracking numbers for each shipment
  • No additional shipping charges for split shipments

13. Customs and Duties

For shipments to U.S. territories:

  • No customs fees for most U.S. territories
  • Some territories may have local taxes or fees
  • Customer is responsible for any local taxes or import fees
  • These fees are not included in your order total

14. Shipping Guarantee

While we work hard to meet delivery estimates, shipping times are not guaranteed due to:

  • Carrier delays beyond our control
  • Weather conditions and natural disasters
  • Incorrect shipping addresses
  • Customs delays (for territories)

We are not liable for delays caused by carriers or circumstances beyond our control.
