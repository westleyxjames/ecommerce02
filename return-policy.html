Return Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

At Lunaris Shop, your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a comprehensive 30-day return policy to ensure you're completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll make it right.

1. Our Return Promise

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all eligible products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

2. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Timeframe: Returned within 30 days of delivery date
  • Condition: Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Tags: Original tags must be attached and intact
  • Packaging: Items should be in original packaging when possible
  • Proof of Purchase: Order number or receipt must be provided

3. Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Intimate apparel and undergarments
  • Earrings and pierced jewelry
  • Cosmetics and beauty products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Gift cards
  • Items that show signs of wear or damage

4. How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return your purchase:

1

Contact Us

Email us at return@lunarisshop.online with:

  • Your order number
  • Item(s) you wish to return
  • Reason for return (optional but helpful)
2

Receive Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours (usually much faster). No shipping costs for you!

3

Pack Your Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging or a suitable box. Attach the return label to the outside of the package.

4

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any authorized carrier location (USPS, UPS, FedEx). Keep your tracking number for reference.

5

Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

5. Refund Process

Processing Time

  • Returns are inspected within 2 business days of receipt
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after inspection
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear

Refund Amount

  • Full purchase price of returned item(s)
  • Original shipping costs (if order was over $50 and qualified for free shipping)
  • No restocking fees or hidden charges

6. Exchanges

We gladly offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors!

How to Exchange

  • Email return@lunarisshop.online with your order number and exchange request
  • We'll send you a prepaid return label
  • We'll ship your exchange item as soon as we receive your return
  • If the replacement item has a different price, we'll refund the difference or send a payment link

7. Damaged or Defective Items

We take quality seriously. If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery at help@lunarisshop.online
  • Include photos of the damage or defect
  • We'll send a replacement immediately or issue a full refund
  • No need to return the damaged item unless requested

8. Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item:

  • Contact us immediately at help@lunarisshop.online
  • We'll arrange for the correct item to be shipped at no cost
  • We'll provide a prepaid label to return the incorrect item

9. Return Shipping

Free Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for all eligible returns within the United States. You won't pay anything for return shipping!

International Returns

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International returns are handled on a case-by-case basis.

10. Lost or Stolen Packages

If your package shows as delivered but you didn't receive it:

  • Check with neighbors and household members
  • Look around your property (porches, garages, mailboxes)
  • Contact the carrier with your tracking number
  • Wait 24 hours (sometimes carriers mark items as delivered early)
  • Contact us at help@lunarisshop.online - we'll help resolve the issue

11. Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance" are not eligible for return or exchange unless they arrive damaged or defective. These items are clearly marked on the product page.

12. Store Credit

As an alternative to a refund, you may choose to receive store credit:

  • Store credit is issued as a unique code
  • No expiration date on store credit
  • Can be used on any future purchase
  • Cannot be redeemed for cash

13. Questions?

If you have any questions about our return policy or need assistance with a return, we're here to help!

  • Customer Service: help@lunarisshop.online
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 9AM - 6PM EST
